How it works

  1. 01

    Hold

    Press and hold the right-Option key. A discreet menu-bar indicator turns on.

  2. 02

    Speak

    Talk naturally. Pause, stumble, restart — Whisper handles real speech, not just dictation.

  3. 03

    Release

    Lift the key. Your transcription is pasted at the cursor in any app — Mail, Slack, your terminal.

Why Murmur

Quietly excellent at one thing.

System-wide

Works in every app — Mail, Notion, VS Code, your terminal. One key, everywhere.

Fast

Under a second from release to text. Powered by Groq — the fastest Whisper inference available.

Yours

Your API key, your account. No middleman, no telemetry, no logs. The Keychain holds it.

Out of the way

Lives in the menu bar. No dock icon, no windows, no notifications you didn't ask for.

Get it

Two minutes to set up.

  1. 1. Download the DMG and drag Murmur to Applications.
  2. 2. Grant Microphone & Accessibility access on first launch.
  3. 3. Paste your free Groq API key when prompted. Done.
